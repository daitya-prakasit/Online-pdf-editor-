Interactive Selection | Drag & Drop | Live Edit | Multi-Language

> System Ready. Upload an image to begin scanning.
HOW TO USE:
1. Upload an image. Wait for the OCR scan to complete.
2. Detected words will have a dashed green box around them.
3. Click on any dashed box to unlock it. The system will mask the original text and give you an editable object.
4. Double-Click the unlocked text to type your own (Hindi/English).
5. Drag it anywhere. Change its color and size from the top panel.
